Apply

Apply only if your grade point average is at least a B at the end of your Junior year in high school and your parents combined adjusted gross income, as shown on their most recent federal tax filings, is $50,000.00 or less. Federal tax filings need not be submitted with the application, but please show proof of parents adjusted gross income to your interviewer.
