About

LAS Scholarship program plans to award five scholarships every year to local area high school graduating seniors with a need for financial assistance and have a potential to complete a college degree program. The five scholarships will include one from each of the following institutions; Hope Church, Abundant Faith Church, Springfield High School, Southeast High School, and Lanphier High School in Springfield, Illinois.

Each selected applicant from two churches will receive $2000.00 per year; must be attending their respective churches at least one year and does not necessarily have to graduate from one of the three high schools named above. Each selected applicant from the three named high schools will receive $1000.00 per year.

Each of the scholarship may be renewed for three consecutive years. Applicants planning to attend a Junior college may apply too. In this case the scholarship may be renewed for one consecutive year. For scholarship to be renewed, recipient must have his/her college submit directly to the LAS Scholarship Program his/her grade report certifying that at the end of an academic year the recipient took required number of total credit hours and has a passing grade point average. The submission must be done on line and by September 1 of the current academic year.
